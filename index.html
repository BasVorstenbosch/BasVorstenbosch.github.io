Aston Martin Vulcan

This is a project I worked on for 6 months containing a showcase video of a fully CG Aston Martin Vulcan. I modelled and UV'd the car in Maya, textured in Cinema4D Octane and Substance Painter and Rendered and animated in Cinema4D Octane.

The model was created from scratch using a blueprint I found online. During this project I improved my highpoly modelling skills a lot and go the change to go in dept with Maya much deeper.

Besides improving my 3D modelling skill this is the first every project I did with Cinema4D and Octane. I learned about material creation, animations, lighting and composistioning. My main goal was to recreate a realisic image and I think I got quite close.

Fresh Avacado

The Fresh Avacado game is the first ever 3D project I worked on. This game was made using Maya, Substance Painter and Unity. This game is a quest through caves and dungeons to find the Freshest Avacado! We made this adventure/puzzle game with a team consisting out of 3 artist and 2 programmers. We(the artist team) divided the creation of the environment up in 3 sections, then each artist was responsible for his own section.

As a member of the artist team I made the cave level section showed on this video on the left. All assets in this level and the level design it self were made by me. I tried to get the maximum out of all our mechanics by combining them together as much as I could.

During this project I also got in touch with rigging for the first time. This video on the left shows the result from Maya.

The Forester

The Forester is a 3D game project I worked on during the 2nd year of my education. During this project I worked as the team leader of a team consisting out of 8 people. During this project I was focussed on planning and team dynamics more then asset creation, but I still managed to add my input. This project was made using Maya, Substance Painter, Unity and F-mod.

The gameplay trailer shown above was recorded and edited by me.

I was also responsible for the animations in the game. I animated the main character and all the enemies. This video shows a reel of these animations.
